How to buy from us

We sell via direct sales, and via ebay.

For direct sales, ring or email us your enquiry or order. See the contact us page to find where.

A small selection of our stock is listed on ebay. To find us, please put ‘bsf bolts’ into the search box, (not our ID or store name).

Payment

Payment by cheque or postal order is available, and goods can be sent worldwide. (Payment by cheque is unavailable for overseas orders, we are based in Australia).

Please note that prices can differ between direct sales and ebay sales (due to fees, specials, etc).
