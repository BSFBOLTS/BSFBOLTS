  • Vintage and Classic Car's
  • Vintage Motorcycles
  • Vintage Aircraft
  • Old and rare machinery
  • Also lots of items for model engineering

Our Stock

This is a general idea of our stock – not all sizes or combinations are available.

BSF

BSF bolts, setscrews, screws, nuts, locknuts, with head types including hex, cheesehead, countersunk, roundhead, mushroom head, with slotted, Philips and Allen drives, in a variety of materials, tensile strengths, and plating's, with diameters ranging from 3/16” to 1 ¼”, and lengths ranging from ¼” to 6”. We also have a range of engineering taps and dies, including some rare left hand thread.

BA

BA screws in a range of head types, and nuts, from size 0 to size 10, as well as engineering taps to size 12.

BSW

BSW small variety of items.

Other Items

We also have some items in brass thread and cycle thread, as well as a range of flat and spring washers, and some miscellaneous hardware such as clevis pins and split pins. Many of our items are from military or aircraft sources, and some have a NSN code (NATO Stock Number) for exact identification.
